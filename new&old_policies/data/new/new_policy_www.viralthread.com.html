Cookies

 

A cookie is a snippet of information sent from a website’s server that is saved by your browser. When you request a page from that website’s server the stored information will be sent back to the website’s server. This enables the website’s server to track and identify the web browser.

 

Cookies can be either “session” cookies or “persistent” cookies. A session cookie will expire at the end of a user session, when the browser is closed. Persistant cookies are stored by the browser and will remain valid until its defined expiry date, unless they are deleted by the user

 

We use both session and persistent cookies on this website.
