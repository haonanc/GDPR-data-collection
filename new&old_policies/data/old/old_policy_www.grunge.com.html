Privacy Policy

Privacy

Grunge.com provides this Privacy Policy to inform users of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personally identifiable information received from users of Grunge's website, located at the domain Grunge.com (the "Grunge Website"). Please note that this Privacy Policy only applies to the Grunge Website.

Information Collection and Use

It is not necessary to provide Grunge with your personally identifiable information to use the Grunge Website, and Grunge does not collect personally identifiable information in connection with the operation of the Grunge Website.  If you contact Grunge by email through the Grunge Website, Grunge may keep a record of your contact information and correspondence, and may use your email address, and any information that you provide to Grunge in your message, to respond to you.  Grunge treats this information as it would treat any other unsolicited business correspondence, and has no obligation to keep this information confidential, nor to take any security measures to protect this information.

When you visit the Grunge Website, Grunge's servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type or the domain from which you are visiting. For most users accessing the Internet from an Internet service provider, the IP address will be different every time you log on. Grunge uses Log Data to monitor use of the Grunge Website and the services we offer via the Grunge Website and for the Grunge Website's technical administration.

Cookies and Other Analytic Tools

Like many websites, Grunge also uses "cookie" technology to collect additional website usage data and to improve the Grunge Website and the services offered via the Web Site. A cookie is a small data file that Grunge's servers transfer to your computer's hard disk. Grunge may also use tracking pixels. Tracking pixels are small graphic images, which are used in email messages to determine whether the messages were opened and the links were clicked, or are used on the Grunge Website to collect information about the browser and device viewing the Grunge Website. Grunge does not use cookies or tracking pixels to collect personally identifiable information.

Grunge mainly uses "session cookies", which enable certain features of the Grunge Website and services offered via the Grunge Website, to better understand how you interact with the Web Site and services we offer via the Grunge Website, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on the Grunge Website, and to improve the Grunge Website and services offered via the Grunge Website. Session cookies should be deleted from your computer when you disconnect from or leave the Grunge Website.

Grunge also uses a persistent cookie that stays on your computer after you leave the Grunge Website, for the sole purpose of ensuring that you receive a variety of Grunge content (also called "frequency-capping").

Third Party Advertisers

The Grunge Website includes advertising from third party advertisers. Grunge does not provide any personally identifiable information to these advertisers.

Third party advertisers, or companies working on their behalf, may serve directly to your browser the advertisements that appear on the Grunge Website, which allows them to receive Log Data (as defined above). They may also use cookies, JavaScript, tracking pixels and other technologies to measure the effectiveness of their ads and to personalize advertising content. Grunge does not control any third party advertiser tracking or measurement technologies, and the information practices of third party advertisers are not covered by this Privacy Policy. Please contact them directly for more information about their privacy practices.

Opting Out

Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by editing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. If you choose not to receive tracking pixels, you will need to disable HTML images in your email program and browser, but that may affect images in other emails you receive and the web pages you browse.

Grunge also uses analytics technologies such as Google Analytics, Quantcast and comScore. For Google's privacy practices see www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html and to opt out of data recording and analysis by Google Analytics on the Grunge Website, see https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. For Quantcast's privacy practices see https://www.quantcast.com/privacy/, and to opt out of receiving interest-based content and ads enabled by Quantcast, see https://www.quantcast.com/opt-out/. For comScore's privacy practices see http://www.comscore.com/About-comScore/Privacy-Policy and http://www.scorecardresearch.com/privacy.aspx, and to opt out of having comScore associate site visits with your browser, see http://www.scorecardresearch.com/preferences.aspx.

Do Not Track

Some web browsers may transmit "do not track" signals to the websites and other online services with which the browser communicates. Grunge currently does not take action in response to these signals. Also, please note that regardless of Grunge's practices with respect to "do not track" signals, third party service providers Grunge has engaged may use cookies to collect your personal information or online activities over time and across different web sites. For more information regarding Do Not Track mechanisms, see http://allaboutdnt.com/.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Grunge cooperates with government and law enforcement officials or private parties, to enforce and comply with the law. Grunge may disclose any information about you to government or law enforcement officials or private parties as Grunge, in its sole discretion, believes necessary or appropriate to respond to claims, legal process (including subpoenas), to protect the property and rights of Grunge or a third party, the safety of the public or any person, to prevent or stop any illegal, unethical, or legally actionable activity, or to comply with the law.

If any or all of the assets of Grunge are acquired by, or merged with another entity, Grunge will share with this entity some or all of the information collected through the Grunge Website.

Security

Because Grunge does not collect any personally identifiable information from the Grunge Website, Grunge does not take any specific security measures to protect the information we do collect.

Links to Other Sites

The Grunge Website may contain links to other web sites ("Linked Sites") operated by Grunge or third parties. Linked Sites may place their own cookies or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit personal information from you. This Privacy Policy addresses only the use and disclosure of information that Grunge collects through the Grunge Website. Other sites follow different rules regarding the use or disclosure of the personal information you submit to them. Grunge does not exercise control over third party Linked Sites. Your use of any Linked Site operated by Grunge is subject to the terms and conditions provided by Grunge for such Linked Site.  Grunge encourages you to read the privacy policies or statements of the other websites you visit.

Our Policy Towards Children

The Grunge Website is not directed to children under 18. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided Grunge with personally identifiable information without their consent, he or she should contact Grunge at staff@Grunge.com.  If Grunge becomes aware that a child under 13 has provided Grunge with personally identifiable information, Grunge will remove such information from our files.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Grunge reserves the right to change this policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes to this policy will be posted here.  You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes. If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at staff@Grunge.com.
