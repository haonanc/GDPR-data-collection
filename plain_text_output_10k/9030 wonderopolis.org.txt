Privacy Policy
The National Center for Families Learning respects your privacy. Any information gathered on the site is for the express purpose of determining how families are using the site to further learning together. You may always choose whether to enter any personal information. Any information collected will not be sold, under any circumstances, to any third party.
The information we collect on our site is only used to recognize you as a Wonder friend and make your experience on Wonderopolis more enjoyable. In most cases, the information we collect on Wonderopolis is non-personal (such as first name only, age, and state). If you submit content that is posted on Wonderopolis, you will be identified on our site only first name, age, and city and state. When we do ask for personal information (such as an email address), it is optional and used only to enable and maintain a safe environment for all learners. We follow Federal regulations and For example, if you submit a Wonder nomination and we know you are under the age of 13, we will ask permission from your parent or guardian to publish any comment you leave about what inspired your Wonder. Your parent or guardian may request to have any personal information corrected or deleted by sending an email to hello@wonderopolis.org.
