ParaChat Group takes your privacy seriously. To learn more about ParaChat Group's privacy  policy, please read our policy below.
Privacy Policy Coverage
This Privacy Policy covers ParaChat Group's treatment of personally identifiable  information that ParaChat Group collects when you are on the ParaChat.com Web site,  and when you use ParaChat Group's products and/or services. This policy also covers  ParaChat Group's treatment of any personally identifiable information that ParaChat  Group's business partners share with ParaChat Group or that ParaChat Group may collect  on a partner or third-party site.
This policy does not apply to the content or practices of companies or Web sites  that ParaChat Group does not own or control, or to people that ParaChat Group  does not employ or manage.
Information Collection and Use
ParaChat Group collects personally identifiable information when you use certain  ParaChat Group products and/or services, when you visit ParaChat Group web pages,  and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. ParaChat Group may also receive  personally identifiable information from our business partners.
When you license free ParaChat Group products, we ask for your email address,  and for permission to fulfill your requests via email for certain products and  services, to contact you about specials and new products, and/or to fulfill your  requests for third-party promotions. By providing this information to ParaChat  Group, you may be limiting your anonymity to us.
When you license paid ParaChat Group products, we ask for your name, address,  zip code, email address, and credit card information. Once you provide this  information to ParaChat Group, you are not anonymous to us.  Information provided  with regard to paid ParaChat Group products is retained for internal use only,  and is not shared with any third party.  As a customer, you may receive product  and service updates directly from ParaChat Group, or from third parties if you  have provided consent to do so.
ParaChat Group also automatically receives and records information on our server  logs from your browser, including your IP address, when you visit our Web site,  or utilize our hosted products.
ParaChat Group uses information for three general purposes: to  fulfill your requests for certain products and services, to contact  you about specials and new products, and to fulfill third-party  promotional material that you may have requested.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
ParaChat Group will not sell or rent your personally identifiable  information to anyone.  ParaChat Group may send personally identifiable  information about you to other companies or people when:
We have your consent to share the information;
We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested;
We need to send the information to companies who work on behalf of ParaChat Group to  provide a product or service to you.
We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or
We find that your actions on our web site, or by using a hosted product,  violate the License Agreement or Guidelines to which you have agreed, or  to which you were requested to adhere, for specific products or services.

Cookies
ParaChat Group may set and access ParaChat Group cookies on your computer.  ParaChat Group  allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and  access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to  their own privacy policies, and not ParaChat Group's Privacy Policy. Advertisers or other  companies do not have access to ParaChat Group's cookies.
Third Party Cookies
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may  place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser. If you choose to click on a drop-down offer or advertisement, an affiliate cookie will be placed on your browser, and you will be re-directed to the partner's or advertiser's website.
Editing and Deleting Your Product and/or Account Information
To edit and/or delete your product and/or account information that has already  been provided by you to ParaChat Group, including whether you want ParaChat Group  to contact you about specials and new products, please contact our Support Team for instructions.  You may also follow the instructions for editing and/or deleting product and/or  account information that were provided to you when your service was initiated, or for mailing list  addition or deletion using the instructions that are included in a mailing list email that you may  have received.
Security
Your security when ordering ParaChat Group products is primary concern.  In certain areas, ParaChat Group uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to  protect data transmissions. Our secure server is called "secure.parachat.com",  Organization = M Square, Inc., Organizational Unit = ParaChat. You may verify  that the form you are using is a secure form, and is consistent with the site  that you are visiting, by clicking on the Secure Site icon on each of ParaChat  Group's secure order forms and Web pages.
Information pertaining to certain ParaChat hosted products is password-protected for your privacy and security.
Policy for Individuals Under the Age of 13
ParaChat is intended for individuals age 13 and over only. We do not provide content  for children. Parents should closely monitor content visited by children, and review  privacy policies on all Web sites to determine whether content is acceptable for children.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
