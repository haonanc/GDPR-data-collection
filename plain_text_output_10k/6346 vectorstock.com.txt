How do we protect your information?
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information. Your account password is stored using a number of encryption methods including a One-way Hashing system. If you lose your password it must be reset as it can not be sent to you via email due to the encryption. Similarly when you log into VectorStock.com your details are passed via. Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption to protect your information.
When placing an order all supplied information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such Banking systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.
After a transaction, your credit cards information will not be stored on our servers.
How to view and/or update your stored information?
You have the ability to update or delete your personal information at any time in the account section. These controls allow you to edit your personal details as well as modify/delete any stored credit card references.
Upon request VectorStock can provide all the information that we hold on you. Please email admin@vectorstock.com with these requests.
Do we use cookies or similar tracking technology?
Cookies are small data files that are stored in your browser (if you allow) that capture and remember information.
We use cookies and other similar technology to save browsing prefences such as image layout size and your cart and gallery contents when logged in. We also track visitor events anonymously (clicks, purchases, downloads) to improve our image search and related services. No personally identifiable information is collected with these events.
Currently our site uses a small set of third-party services which may use tracking for analytics or advertising services. These include the following; New Relic (Analytics), Google AdWords (Advertising), Google Analytics (Analytics) and CrazyEgg (Analytics). For more information about third-party services and the option to opt-out from tracking, you can visit http://optout.networkadvertising.org or http://youronlinechoices.eu .
