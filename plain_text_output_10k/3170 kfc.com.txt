KFC AUSTRALIA PRIVACY POLICY
1. About this policy
Kentucky Fried Chicken Pty Ltd (ABN 79 000 587 780) and KFC Adco Limited (ABN 22 083 047 992) and each of its independently owned franchisees (“KFC Franchisees”) (collectively referred to as “KFC”, “we”, “us” or “our”) are committed to protecting the privacy of personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) (“Privacy Laws”). This Privacy Policy sets out our approach to handling personal information and includes information about how we collect, use, disclose and hold your personal information.
The scope of this Privacy Policy covers the personal information handling practices of restaurants owned by KFC in Australia and KFC Franchisees. Kentucky Fried Chicken Pty Ltd and KFC Adco Limited do not control the use or disclosure of personal information and may not have access to the personal information held by KFC Franchisees. KFC Franchisees are responsible for ensuring that your personal information collected by them is handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Australian Privacy Principles.
This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected in any way through our company owned and independently owned restaurants, restaurant support centre, customer service centres, using a computer or mobile device, through our website (www.kfc.com.au) (“Website”). You should read this Privacy Policy carefully because anytime you provide your personal information to us or obtain our goods or services, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
We may, from time to time, review and update this Privacy Policy. A current version of this Privacy Policy will be published on our Website, or may be obtained free of charge upon request to the contact details below. All personal information collected and held by us will be governed by the most recently updated Privacy Policy.
2. Collection of personal information by KFC
We will only collect personal information from you that we reasonably require for one or more of our business functions or activities, and will do so by lawful and fair means.
The kinds of personal information we may collect from you will depend on what type of interaction you have with us. However, it may include:
information such as your name, postal address, date of birth, gender, occupation, telephone number, email address, age, IP address and credit or debit account details;
personal information you provide to us when you participate in a promotion, competition, promotional activity, loyalty program, survey, market research or customer behavioural activity, or subscribe to our mailing list,
personal information obtained through use of our Website, or interacting with or by following our social media pages like Facebook, Twitter and Instagram;
personal information you provide to us via a KFC branded restaurant, customer service centre, or support centre, or when you place an order in-store, by telephone, or via KFC’s mobile or electronic ordering system. This information may include your name and contact details, menu preferences and previous order information, IP address and financial information including credit card details and payment details;
your records of communication with us when you submit an enquiry or make a complaint;
if you are applying for a position with us, your personal information as outlined in the section below entitled “Employment Applications”;
if you are applying to become a KFC Franchisee, your personal information as outlined in the section below entitled “Franchise Applications”.
If you interact with us through our Website or a mobile application we may also collect personal information about you including:
usage and preference information, including by the use of cookies which is detailed further below in the section entitled “Cookies and passive information collection”;
information about the device you are using;
other information including your IP address, access dates and times, browser type and pages visited.
In some circumstances, we may collect personal information from third parties such as credit reporting agencies or our marketing agencies. Where we do, we will ensure that we act in accordance with relevant Australian laws.
You have the option of not identifying yourself, or to use a pseudonym when dealing with us. However, in some cases it will not be practicable to deal with you anonymously or using a pseudonym, including when you have elected to receive direct marketing materials or requested delivery of products or services from us, in which case we may be unable to provide you with materials, products or services if you do not identify yourself.
We will not collect sensitive information about you (such as information about your racial or ethnic origins or political or religious beliefs) unless the collection is required by law or you have consented to such collection including if you are applying for a position with KFC or to become a KFC Franchisee.
3. The purpose of collecting your personal information
We will generally only collect and use your personal information for the primary purposes of:
carrying out general business operations;
providing you with goods and services;
communicating with you;
meeting legal and regulatory obligations;
conducting, improving and developing a relationship with you; and
marketing (such as providing you with information about KFC products and promotional notices and offers).
4. How your personal information is held
Where we store your personal information depends on the interaction you have had with us. However, some areas may include:
databases for processing customer enquiries or feedback;
or email or SMS databases for marketing communications.
We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from loss or unauthorised access. Unfortunately no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed as totally secure. Although we strive to protect such information, we do not warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our online services and you use these services at your own risk.
We only keep your personal information for as long as it is required for the purpose for which it was collected or as otherwise required by law. We will take appropriate measures to destroy or permanently de-identity your personal information if there is no longer need to retain it. These measures may vary depending on the type of information concerned, the way it was collected and how it was stored.
5. How your personal information will be used
We use your personal information for our business functions and activities, and to provide products and services to you, which may include:
processing meal orders you place with us in our branded restaurants or via our electronic or mobile ordering system, providing you with email or SMS confirmation of your meal orders, providing you with our products and services and processing meal refunds where applicable;
locating the nearest restaurant to you and providing you with location based notifications;
administering and responding to your enquiry or feedback about our products and/or services;
conducting, and allowing you to participate in, a KFC promotion, competition, promotional activity, loyalty program, survey, market research or customer behavioural activity;
providing marketing and advertising materials for current and future products and services, invitations to events and special offers, competitions and promotions in person and through other means including our Website, telephone, mail, electronic messages (e.g. SMS, MMS, instant messaging and email) or any other form of electronic, emerging, digital or conventional communication;
analysing our products and services so as to improve and develop new products and services;
assisting you with remembering and re-ordering from our menu in future;
administering and conducting traineeships;
facilitating our internal business operations, including fulfilment of any legal and regulatory requirements;
maintaining and improving the operation or navigation of our Website;
processing and considering your employment application and conducting reference checks (refer to Employment Application section below);
providing information that you request about our franchises and processing and considering your franchise application and conducting record checks (refer to Franchise Application section below).
We operate CCTV cameras at our premises and restaurants.  Images captured by our CCTV cameras are collected, used and stored for security and operational purposes, and to assist with any investigations relating to incidents or accidents at our premises and restaurants.
We may also use your personal information for other purposes not listed above which will be made clear to you at the time we collect your personal information, or for such purposes as may be required or permitted by law.
6. Disclosure of personal information to third parties
We may disclose personal information we collect from you:
to related companies, KFC Franchisees (or to KFC and its related companies if a KFC Franchisee collects the personal information), suppliers, consultants, contractors, and agents for the purposes set out in the section above entitled “How your personal information will be used” or for other purposes related to the purpose for which the personal information is collected and which you would reasonably expect us to do so.  For example, your name and telephone number may be disclosed to our supplier to enable that supplier to respond to your request for information about a particular product it produces;
to relevant Federal, State, Territory food safety authorities and health authorities for the purpose of investigating a food safety or health issue;
if you are an employee or trainee, to Federal and State educational training institutions for the purpose of conducting retail training and receiving funding for such training;
to KFC Franchisees, including those located in Australia and New Zealand, if you have made a enquiry or complaint relating to a store that is owned and operated by one of our KFC Franchisees;
to third parties to help us analyse our business practices and improve our products and services, and obtain feedback;
to third party service providers that we use to help us conduct our business (including but not limited to technology service providers, archival service providers and marketing and communications agencies and service providers);
to our professional advisors and insurers in the course of obtaining professional advice;
in the event there is a change of ownership of any KFC business, to the new owners of the business (but only on the basis that the personal information is kept within the business);
where the law requires or authorises us to do so;
to others that you have been informed of at the time any personal information is collected from you; and
with your consent (express or implied) to others.
If we engage third party contractors to perform services for us which involves handling personal information, we will require our contractors to comply with Privacy Laws.

Your personal information may also be processed by, or disclosed to employees, representatives, or other third parties operating outside of Australia who work for, or are engaged by KFC or its affiliated companies in other countries, including in the USA and in other countries where KFC operates, and to our services providers who may be located outside of Australia, including in Singapore.

Before your personal information is disclosed to an overseas recipient, we will take steps that are reasonable in the circumstances to ensure that the overseas recipient does not breach Australian Privacy Laws in relation to your personal information.

11. Cookies and passive information collection
We use technologies such as cookies to personalise your experience on our Website. For example, we use cookies to recognise your browser on each visit to our Website, to track pages visited on our site and to display KFC advertisements. Cookies are small files that our web server may send to your computer or device when you visit our Website.  Cookies can facilitate your ongoing access to and use of the Website.  They allow us to store your preferences, collect information about how you visit and access our Website, track usage patterns and to compile data that can assist us improve our content, products, services and target advertising.  Most often, they are used to recognise repeat users of our Website and to remember user preferences.
We may use cookies to help us improve our products and services.
Most browsers are automatically set up to accept cookies. The storage of cookies can be de-activated or the browser can be configured in a way that sends a notification when cookies are sent. You are always free to decline our cookies. However, certain features of the Website may not work without cookies and this may limit the services provided by our Website.
For more information about cookies and how you can opt out, you can visit http://www.youronlinechoices.com.au/.
Third party data and advertising companies may use cookies to collect information about your online activities, including visits to our Website. They use this information to try to match advertising you see on other, non-KFC websites you visit, with your interests.
We also may use technologies called beacons that communicate information from your device to a server. Beacons can be embedded in online content, videos, and emails, and can allow a server to read certain types of information from your device, know when you have viewed particular content or a particular email message, determine the time and date on which you viewed the beacon, and the IP address of your device. We and other third parties use beacons for a variety of purposes, including to analyse the use of our Website and (in conjunction with cookies) to provide content and ads that are more relevant to you.
To the extent that you are using any of our location-based services or functionality, then your location data including the location of your device upon using such location-based services will be handled in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy.
We also collect and use your IP address to help diagnose any problems with our server and administer our Website.  Your IP address is used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information about you.
