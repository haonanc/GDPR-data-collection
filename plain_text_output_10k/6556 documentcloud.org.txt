DocumentCloud is not intended as an anonymous publishing service. All Document Contributors must be associated with an organization or publication. As a Document Contributor, you are required to provide DocumentCloud with your full name. Your name and the name of your organization or publication may be made public alongside any documents you publish through DocumentCloud.
