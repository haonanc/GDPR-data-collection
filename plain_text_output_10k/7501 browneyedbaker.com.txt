Below is the privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) applicable to your use of BrownEyedBaker.com (the “Web Site”). The Privacy Policy affects your rights as an individual user, as well as the rights of Brown Eyed Baker, LLC (“Brown Eyed Baker”).
Brown Eyed Baker is committed to maintaining the privacy and protection of your personal information. This Privacy Policy tells you about the information we collect on our Web Site, what we do with that information, and with whom we share the information. In order to ensure your privacy, information Brown Eyed Baker collects from visitors to its Web Site is used only in the manner and for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. By using the Web Site, you are consenting to the information collection and use practices described in this Privacy Policy, which may be modified from time to time.
Information Collected
The information gathered falls into two categories: (1) information you supply when you register to receive emails or comment on the Web Site, and (2) tracking information collected as you navigate through the Web Site.
Personally Identifying Information
In operating the Web Site, Brown Eyed Baker may utilize registration, comment forms, online surveys, and other online forms that ask users to provide their names, addresses, e-mail addresses, and/or telephone numbers (“Personal Information”).
Agreement to Collect, Use and Disclose Personal Information
Your provision of Personal Information to Brown Eyed Baker and/or this Web Site means that you agree that we may collect, use and disclose your Personal Information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, you should not provide any Personal Information. Unfortunately, certain services can only be offered if you provide Personal Information to us. Consequently, if you choose not to provide us with any required Personal Information, we may not be able to offer you those services.
Tracking Information
Cookies. From time to time, the Web Site may use the standard “cookies” feature of major browser applications, which feature stores on your computer a small piece of data about your visit to our Web Site. The Web Site does not set any Personal Information in cookies, nor does it employ any data-capture mechanisms on the Web Site other than cookies. Cookies shed light on which areas of the Web Site are useful and which areas need improvement. You can choose whether to accept cookies by changing the settings on your browser. However, if you choose to disable this function, your experience at the Web Site may be diminished and some features may not work as they were intended.
Click Tracking. As is true of most websites, Brown Eyed Baker gathers certain information automatically and stores it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the Web Site, track users’ movements around the Web Site and gather demographic information about the user base as a whole. Except as noted herein, this automatically-collected data is not linked to Personal Information.
IP Addresses. Each user’s IP address is logged with each visit and each action performed on the Web Site. This information is never provided to third parties, except as otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy, and is logged only for a temporary period of time. IP address information is linked to Personal Information and may be used in an effort to block a “banned” user from accessing the Web Site.
Advertising. Brown Eyed Baker uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web Site. These companies may use information (not including your Personal Information) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
Participation on a Public Website. You should be aware that any Personal Information you submit to the comments section of the Web Site may be read, collected, or used by other users of this Web Site, including anonymous visitors.

Personal Information. Your Personal Information will not be shared with any third parties without your consent, except as necessary to provide you with the services offered by us or to comply with the law. Personal Information may be disclosed if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to (1) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on the Web Site, (2) protect and defend the rights or property of Brown Eyed Baker or the users of this Web Site, or (3) act under circumstances to protect the safety of the public or users of this Web Site.
