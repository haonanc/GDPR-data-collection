Cookies are usually account identifiers and user preferences that allow you to speed through the log-in process by automatically providing the data so that you do not have to type it out and tailors your online experience in general, including content and/or advertising that is shown to you on our Website and other websites. Some Texas Roadhouse pages may use cookies so that we may better serve you when you return to our Website and to offer you certain services. Texas Roadhouse also tracks the domains from which our Website is visited and uses that data to analyze for trends and statistics.
You can set your web browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the option to accept or reject it, or to refuse all cookies. You may not be able to conduct some transactions or use some services on our Website if you disable your web browser from accepting cookies.
