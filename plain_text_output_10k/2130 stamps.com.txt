Your data rights and choices
The following is a summary of choices you can make to exercise your data rights:
You have the right to request cancelling any promotional communication using the link at the bottom of the message. You can also contact our customer support to have be removed from our promotional contact list. Please note that, even if you cancel promotional communications, you will continue to receive non-promotional, transactional messages regarding your account.
You have the right to access and edit your profile and billing information at any time. To update your information, log in to your FindYourStampsValue.com account and or payment processor account. If you require assistance, please contact us.
Right to be forgotten
You have the right to be forgotten which means that, at any time, you can request that FindYourStampsValue.com permanently delete all data records and logs, emails or text and photo search requests, payment all the other information. In these cases we will need to retain partial information to fulfil our responsibilities before the payment processor.
Access under 16 years of age
Our services are not directed at children. Furthermore, we do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 16 years of age, unless consent is given or authorised by the holder of parental responsibility over the child. If we become aware that someone under 16 has provided us with personal details, we will take steps to delete such information. If you become aware that a child has unlawfully or unwittingly provided us with personal data, please contact us through the contact form.
Request to stop using your information
Even if you have previously consented to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, you have the right, at any time, to change your mind and object to the collection, use, and processing of your personal information. Additionally, you are under no contractual obligation to continue to provide any information to FindYourStampsValue.com. However, we require certain information in order to provide you with our services. Therefore, if you disagree with the terms of this Privacy Policy or our Terms of Service, you should stop using FindYourStampsValue.com, and contact us so that we may delete your information.
