We at Watchuseek B.V.  are committed to protecting the privacy of visitors and registered users of the  website Watchuseek.com and do our very best to ensure that any personal data is  held securely and treated with the utmost care. How we use  it. If you choose to provide us with your e-mail address  or other (indirect) personal data, we will process such data in a proper and  careful manner and only for legitimate purposes, such as handling your  registration, enabling moderators to effectively moderate the forums, contacting you with regard to your use of any of the forums,  or to respond to your inquiry. At  all times, we will process your personal data in accordance with Dutch Data  Protection Act.
Cookies. To  increase the ease of use of our website, we use “cookies”.  A cookie  is a tiny text file that is left on your computer, which makes it possible for  us to recognize your computer when you visit Watchuseek.com again. You are  always entitled to refuse cookies. To do so you can adjust your browser  settings to disable all or certain cookies. Please note however that if you  disable cookies, some services may not be available to you.

These companies  may use information (NOT including your name, address, email  address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in  order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If  you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices  about not having this information used by these companies, click here .
Access to your  personal data. Registered users may review, update, or delete  personal data collected by us by clicking on the appropriate link on the  website. In general, if you wish to have access to your personal data to  correct or delete the information that we have collected about you, please feel  free to send us an email detailing your request and the data  you have provided to us.
