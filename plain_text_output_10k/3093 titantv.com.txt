Gathering of Information
TITANTV may collect personally-identifying information from TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application for a variety of purposes. Such personally identifying information may include, but is not limited to, your name, address, and e-mail address.
TITANTV may ask you to provide certain personal information when you register for certain features or services included in TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application. TITANTV also may ask you to provide personal information in other limited circumstances specified at the time the information is gathered. Any information that you submit on or through TitanTV.com or the TitanTV.com Mobile Application may become public.
Like all other Web servers, TITANTV's Web server may automatically create log files for each user who accesses TitanTV.com or the TitanTV.com Mobile Application. These "access logs" may allow TITANTV to make TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application more useful to our users. The access logs may record the identifier provided for your mobile device by your wireless carrier ("carrier identifier"). Otherwise, the access logs do NOT record a user's name, address, e-mail address, credit card numbers, or any other personally-identifying information. Rather, they may contain some or all of the following information:
o   The Internet Protocol Address (IP Address) of the device which downloaded, installed or used the Mobile Application.
o   The date of use.
o   The time of use.
o   The path taken through TitanTV.com or the TitanTV.com Mobile Application.
o   The operating system and device type being used.
o   A list of files downloaded or viewed.
o   The amount of time spent listening to/viewing media files.
o   Any errors encountered.
Cookies and Other Technologies
TITANTV may also use 'cookies' to collect information. A cookie is a string of characters that can be written to a file on the user's device when the user accesses TitanTV.com or downloads, installs or uses the TitanTV.com Mobile Application.
TITANTV uses cookies to track users' activity through TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application in order to improve the site, the application architecture, to offer the features that would not be possible without them, to offer third party content, and to recall specific information to save the user time during subsequent use of TitanTV.com or the TitanTV.com Mobile Application. For example, cookies allow TITANTV to remember your (and/or your device’s) location. Interactive features may also use cookies. Our sponsorship service vendors, which serve sponsorship messages onto TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application, and third party vendors that measure and analyze the use of TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application for TITANTV, may also use their own cookies.
TITANTV and the third party vendors that measure and analyze use of TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application for TITANTV also may use web beacons, alone or in conjunction with cookies, to help compile information about and facilitate usage of TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application. A web beacon is an electronic image, also known as a single-pixel (1x1) or clear .gif, which can be used to recognize certain information on your device, such as cookies, the time and date of your actions within TitanTV.com or the TitanTV.com Mobile Application, and a description of where content is placed. We may use web beacons on TitanTV.com and the TitanTV.com Mobile Application from time to time for this and other purposes.
