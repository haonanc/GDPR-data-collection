Privacy Policy for Screen Cast Mirroring app
This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Screen Cast Mirroring (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by MOBZAPP. The Application is made to allow users to cast (mirroring) their screen to compatible devices such SmartTVs.

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, anonymous location, device type, operating system version and type, certain device settings, device time zone, device carrier, current IP address, applications installed on the device, and platform-specific advertising identifiers, and information about the way you use the Application.                                      That information may be used by third parties to customize ads in this app or in other apps:  for instance, if your device is often located at or near music venues, you might receive offers for music tickets (even when you are not at a music venue).  The information also may be used for market or other research regarding aggregated traffic patterns:  for instance, a company that analyzes shopping trends might use the information to learn whether more or fewer devices are seen near malls or in other shopping districts.
Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?
When you visit the mobile application, we may use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to determine anonymous physical location of your device in order to determine the city you are located within and display a location map with relevant advertisements. We may share your current location with other users or partners.          If you do not want us to use your location for the purposes set forth above, you can opt out by following instructions described in the "opt out rights" section bellow.

What are my opt-out rights?
There are multiple opt-out options for users of this Application:
EEA users: opt-out of all information collection from application settings option:  If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) you can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by going to application settings under Preferences/About/Data Settings.           From there you can simply withdraw or deny again your consent. You will still see ads, but they may not be as relevant to your interests.
Opt-out of all information collection by uninstalling the Application:  You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application.           You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.
Opt-out from the use of information to serve targeted advertising by advertisers and/or third party network advertisers: you may at any time opt-out from further allowing us to have access to your location data by disabling the location permission for the Application in your device settings or by disabling the location service at device level.
