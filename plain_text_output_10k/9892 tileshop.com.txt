We and our online service providers use cookies to improve your experience on the Web Site and in our stores, the quality our Web Site, and the quality of other web sites of interest to you. We also use cookies to monitor individual activity in aggregate to improve our Web Site and marketing. In addition to cookies, we and our online service providers may use web beacons and other online tracking tools. The information we gather includes IP address, user language, the operating system, browser type, the presence/absence of “flash” plug-ins, screen resolution, connection type, and information that identifies the cookie. For example, we use cookies and other online tracking tools to:
Remember what items are in your Shopping Cart
Track new visitors to the Web Site
Collect geographic data determined by the IP address of your computer
Learn what site referred you to our Web Site
Recognize you when you return to our Web Site
Store your password if you have an account at tileshop.com
Enable you to use an expedited checkout, if you have created an account at tileshop.com
Study how our customers navigate through our Web Site and which products they request in Web Site searches

Provide you with enhanced online display advertising tailored to your interests. To opt out of this personalized display advertising from Tile Shop, click here .

SECURITY
When you place orders on our websites, all of your order information, including your credit card number and delivery address, is transmitted through the Internet using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. SSL technology causes your browser to encrypt your order information before transmitting it to our secure server. SSL technology, an industry standard, is designed to prevent someone other than operators of our websites from capturing and viewing your personal information.
While we use industry standard means to protect our websites and your information, the Internet is not 100% secure. The measures we use are appropriate for the type of information we collect. We cannot promise that your use of our websites or mobile applications will be completely safe. We encourage you to use caution when using the Internet. Online access to your personal information is protected with a password you select. We strongly recommend that you do not share your password.
