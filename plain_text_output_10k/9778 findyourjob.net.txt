How to Opt Out
a. If for any reason you no longer wish to be contacted by Company, please send an email to         privacy@findyourjob.net requesting that you be removed from Company’s marketing communications mailing list or         follow the “Unsubscribe” link in an email from Company.
b. Even if you have opted out of Company’s marketing communications, you still need to go through a separate         process to unsubscribe from any SMS alerts you previously signed up to receive. You can unsubscribe from these         alerts by using the “STOP” function within those messages.
c. If you wish to opt out of being contacted by a third party service provider, you will need to contact such         third party service provider separately to request the removal of your information from its database.
