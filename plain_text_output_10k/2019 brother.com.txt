2. How We Use Your Personal Information and the Basis on Which We Use It
If you are a customer we use your personal information to:
ensure that our records are kept accurate and up to date;
comply with legal obligations to which we are subject;
check and maintain quality control;
carry out analysis of our stock, ensure correct functioning of logistics and check stocks;
provide you with customer service, after sale support and software updates;
ship products to you;
recall products.
If you are a business partner we use your personal information to:
carry out background checks prior to engaging you as a business partner;
contact you with questions and other information regarding the services you provide;
ensure that our records are kept accurate and up to date where you, your employees or contractors work on our facilities;
pay invoices;
comply with legal obligations to which we are subject.
We must have a legal basis to process your personal information. In most cases the legal basis will be one of the following:
to fulfil our contractual obligations to you. If you are a customer our contractual obligations to you include shipping products, providing customer service, after sale support, software updates, and recalling products when necessary. If you are a business partner our contractual obligations to you include paying invoices correctly and ensuring you are able to access our premises when required;
to comply with our legal obligations to you, for example health and safety obligations while you are on our premises, or to a third party (e.g. the taxation authorities); and
to meet our legitimate interests.  If you are a customer our legitimate interests, for example, are maintaining quality control and logistics, carrying out analysis for product development and market research, checking stocks and ensuring that our records are kept up to date and accurate.  If you are a business partner our legitimate interest, for example, are ensuring that the services you provide are appropriate for our needs, that your services function correctly with our systems, that any complaints or concerns can be promptly relayed to you, and our records are kept up to date and accurate.
