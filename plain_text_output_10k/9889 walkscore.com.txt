Walk Score Privacy Policy
By using Walk Score ("WS") you permit the collection and use of your personal information as outlined below in our Privacy Policy.
1. Information We Collect
Information you submit: WS collects information you submit such as addresses searched, profile information including name, email address, and user profile pictures. When you submit photos and comments to WS these will be displayed publicly  along with your name and we may distribute this information to a wider audience through third party sites and services.
Information we collect: Some information collected is not explicitly submitted by the user. For example, we log your browser information, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, and timestamp. We may collect and store information about your location or the location of photographs if you enable your mobile device or computer to send us location information.
2. How We Use Your Information
Information you submit such as photographs and comments including your name may be redisplayed on the Walk Score website, other websites operated by Walk Score, and third party sites. WS will occasionally send email to our users and you have the ability to unsubscribe at any time.
If you fill out a form to contact a real estate professional or call a real estate professional via our mobile apps, we may share personally identifiable information with that real estate professional. This may include data that you input as well as other usage data we collect.
We use Facebook Connect and Google Login to authenticate users and to prevent abuse when people edit Walk Score information. We may use information from your  profile to contact you about Walk Score related features.
Browser-type related information, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, and timestamp data help us diagnose problems with our server and administer Walk Score. In addition, we use this information to perform statistical analyses of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas of the site and to inform advertisers of such information as well as the number of users that have been exposed to, or clicked on, their advertising banners.
