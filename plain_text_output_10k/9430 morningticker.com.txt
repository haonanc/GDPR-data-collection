Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Those who engage in transactions with Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC may display this information publicly or provide it to others. Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC uses cookies to help Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC identify and track visitors, their usage of Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC website, and their website access preferences. Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using our websites, with the drawback that certain features of our websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Ads
Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Jones Kilmartin Group, LLC and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
