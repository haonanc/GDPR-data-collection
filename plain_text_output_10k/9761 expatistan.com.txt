Cookies
We use cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website and perform traffic analysis.         This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse this website.         By using our website, you agree to our use of cookies.
The Website uses cookies to improve user comfort, as allowing them to remember you and your         individual settings and preferences.
A cookie is a simple text file (usually of a few kB) stored on your computer, notebook, or mobile         device by the Website able to find and read the cookie. Virtually all web pages use cookies and these         files are not harmful to your user system.
Every cookie is unique to your web browser. The cookie contains certain information such as unique         identifier, site name, certain digits and numbers.
There are two types of cookies - temporary cookies and permanent cookies.         Temporary cookies are stored on your device (such as a computer, phone ...) until you leave the         Website where they are removed. Such cookies are used by the website to remember information when         switching between pages so that there is no need to re-enter or fill in the information again.         Permanent cookies remain stored in your device even after you leave the website to remember you as a         returning visitor - without identifying a particular person.
Cookies can be disabled by changing settings in your browser. If you choose to disable cookies, you         will not be able to use this website.
If your Internet browser has cookies enabled, you confirm your consent to the use of cookies on the website.
