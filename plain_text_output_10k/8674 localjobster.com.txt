You may choose not to be contacted by LocalJoster by following the "unsubscribe" instructions in the specific email         from which you wish to opt-out or emailing privacy@localjobster.com, specifying the particular communications from         which you wish to be removed.
To opt out of receiving marketing communications via SMS, respond to the text message that you have received with         “Stop”, “Quit”, or “End”.
In addition, under “California’s Shine the Light” law, California residents may opt out of allowing LocalJobster to:         (a) share personally identifiable information with LocalJobster affiliates for their direct marketing purposes; and         (b) share personally identifiable information with third party companies for their marketing purposes. You may         indicate your preferences to opt out of any combination of the choices above by emailing privacy@localjobster.com.         Please specify in your email from which choice you are opting out:
Receiving communications from LocalJobster;
Allowing LocalJobster to share your personal information with LocalJobster affiliates for their marketing purposes; and/or
Allowing LocalJobster to share your personal information with third-party companies for their marketing purposes.
Please note, however, that even if you "opt out" as described above, LocalJobster will not be able to remove your         personal information from the databases of affiliates or third parties with which we have already shared your         personal information. If you wish to stop receiving marketing communications from such affiliates or third parties,         please contact them directly or utilize the opt-out mechanisms set forth in their respective privacy policies or         marketing communications.
