Information about Privacy on MHVillages.com
MHVillages.com is committed to protecting your privacy. You               can visit most pages on our site without giving us any information               about yourself. But sometimes we do need information to provide               services that you request, and this privacy statement explains               data collection and use in those situations. This privacy statement               only applies to MHVillages.com; it does not apply to other               online or offline MHVillages.com sites, products or services.               Please read the complete MHVillages.com privacy statement .
On This Page
Collection of your Personal Information
We will ask you when we need information that personally identifies                 you (personal information) or allows us to contact you. Generally,                 this information is requested when you are registering before,                 ordering e-mail newsletters, or signing up for an event. Personal                 information collected by MHVillages.com often is limited                 to e-mail address and community location, but may include other                 information when need to provide a service you requested.
For example: If you choose a service that requires detailed information,               such as a request for valuation information regarding your home,               we will request personal information necessary for to accurately               assess the value of your home, such as: name, address and telephone               number.
MHVillages.com also collects certain information about your               computer hardware and software. This information may include: your               IP address, browser type, operating system, domain name, access               times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used               for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service,               and to provide general statistics regarding use of MHVillages.com.
MHVillages.com also collects information about which pages               our customers visit within MHVillages.com. This site visitation               data is identified only by a unique ID number, and it is never               linked with personal information.
Use of your Personal Information
We use your personal information for the following purposes:
• To ensure our site is relevant to your needs.
• To deliver services, such as newsletters or events that you request.
• To help us create and publish content most relevant to you.
• To alert you to updated information and other new services from MHVillages.com, if you so request.
• To allow you access to limited-entry areas of our site as appropriate.
At this time, all services are being provided by the volunteers               within Mountain House, including, but not limited to, homeowners               and residents. It may become necessary to utilize the resources               of other companies to provide limited services on our behalf, answering               customer questions about services, sending postal mail and processing               event registration. We will only provide those companies the information               they need to deliver the service, and they are prohibited from               using that information for any other purpose.
MHVillages.com may disclose your personal information if               required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such               action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or               comply with legal process served on MHVillages.com or the               site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of MHVillages.com               and its family of Web sites, or (c) act in urgent circumstances               to protect the personal safety of MHVillages.com employees,               users of MHVillages.com services, or members of the public.
Control of your Personal Information
When you register, or otherwise give us personal information, MHVillages.com               will not share that information with third parties without your               permission, other than for the limited exceptions already listed.               It will only be used for the purposes stated above.
Registering also allows you to tell us how or whether you want               MHVillages.com to communicate with you. Registration will               only be required to receive requested information, such as newsletters               or updates. You will be required to allow us to utilize your personal               information in order to provide the service you have requested.               As stated above, your information will be utilized only in the               manner expressed above. Any changes to this policy will be provided               to you and you will be allowed to request removal of your personal               information should you not agree with the new policy (hereafter               called "opt-out"). Should you request the "opt-out" option,               information will be provided to you instructing you on the process.
Access to your Personal Information
We will provide you with the means to ensure that your personal               information is correct and current.
If you register MHVillages.com or subscribe to a newsletter,               you can review and edit your personal information by sending an               e-mail to the
requesting the changes to, or removal of, all your personal information.
Security of your Personal Information
MHVillages.com is committed to protecting the security of               your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies               and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized               access, use or disclosure. For example, we store the personal information               you provide in computer servers with limited access that are located               in controlled facilities.
Protection of Children's Personal Information
MHVillages.com is a general audience site and does not knowingly               collect any personal information from children.
Use of Cookies
When someone visits the site, a cookie is placed on the visitor's               machine (if the customer accepts cookies) or is read if the visitor               has visited the site previously. One use of cookies is to allow               the auto-login feature of the forum.
If you choose to not have your browser accept cookies from the               MHVillages.com Web site, you will be able to view the text               on the screens, however you will not experience a personalized               visit nor will you be able to subscribe to the service offerings               on the site.
