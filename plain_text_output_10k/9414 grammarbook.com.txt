Types of Information Collected
Information Provided By You
This Site collects personal information online, including names,  postal and e-mail addresses, and billing information, entered by the visitor in  order to join our mailing list or register with the Site. 
Information Collected and Stored Automatically
This Site is not set up to track, collect, or distribute personal  information not entered by visitors. However, as with many websites, the  Site logs do generate certain kinds of non-personally identifiable information,  such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address and the time of your visit, as  well as site usage data (e.g., the number of hits and visits to the site).
Information Collected By Third Party Advertisers
The Site uses Google, a third party vendor, to host customized  advertisements that may be of interest to you, the visitor.
Google  uses cookies to serve ads on the Site. Specifically, Google uses the DART  cookie to serve ads to our visitors based on your visit to the Site  and other sites on the Internet.  When you view an  ad, or click on an ad supported by Google’s advertising services, Google may  set a cookie on your browser.
The use of cookies is fairly standard on many  websites. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. Should you  desire, you can change your browser's settings to either refuse to accept  cookies or notify you when they are placed on your computer. You can also opt out of the use  of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and  content network privacy policy .
