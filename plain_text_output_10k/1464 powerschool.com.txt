1. ​The Personal Information We Collect
We may collect different types of personal and other information based on your use of our products and services and our relationship with you ("Personal Information"). Some examples of Personal Information we may collect include:
We collect information you give us.
Contact Information (such as your name, mailing address, telephone number, and e-mail address);
Information you provide in the context of one of Hooters’ Club memberships (such as favorite Hooters location and your birthday);
Payment information (such as your payment card details, financial account information, security codes, and billing address);
Purchase history (i.e., for Hoot Club members signed up for rewards, who provide purchase information via scanning restaurant receipts);
Information provided in an online job application (such as information about your education and work history and past salaries), or in a franchisee application;
Information provided by you in conjunction with an online sweepstakes entry, a survey, or a promotion;
Other personal information you may provide to us (including photographs).
We collect information through automated methods.
Also, when you visit our Sites or Mobile App, we may collect certain information via automated means (such as cookies, web beacons, and server log files).
For example, our systems may collect information about the following:
• your activity on the Sites and Mobile App (i.e., pages you visit, address of a referring website (if any), identifiers for your mobile device, and Internet Protocol (IP) address) • type of mobile device and its settings • geolocation and current location (if you do not turn off GPS locator on your phone) • communications to us or regarding us on social media • other automatically gathered information when you interact with our Sites or App
We may use this automatically-collected information to help tailor the ads you may see on the Sites and the messaging on the Mobile App. Further, we use this information to operate, maintain, and manage our Sites and Mobile App.
For most mobile devices and computer systems, you are able to withdraw your permission for us to collect this information by using the device or web-browser settings.  You can set your browser to notify you when you receive certain types of cookies, and you can also set your browser to restrict or disable certain types of cookies. Please note that if you do restrict or disable cookies, you may not be able to use all the features of our Sites and Mobile App.
2. ​How We Use The Personal Information We Collect
We may use the Personal Information we collect in a variety of ways, including to:
• Provide you with the best customer experience possible; • Provide the products and services you purchase, and to respond to your questions; • Communicate with you regarding offers, special events, promotions, and sweepstakes; • Deliver customized content and advertising that may be of interest to you; • Address network integrity and security issues; • Locate your nearest Hooters and take/process online food orders; • Create and manage a Hooters Club membership; • Evaluate job applications and respond to your inquiries into potential employment; • Evaluate franchisee applications; • Respond to your requests, questions, and/or comments; • Perform data analyses (including market and consumer research, and trend analyses); • Maintain, manage, and improve our products, marketing, and services.
3. ​Personal Information We Share With Others
Hooters does not sell your Personal Information to unaffiliated third parties.
Hooters may share your personal information with parent companies, subsidiaries and affiliates (Hooters of America Companies, or "HOA Companies"), as well as with Hooters franchisees. Hooters requires these entities to comply fully with this Privacy Policy. Subject to applicable legal restrictions, the HOA Companies may share your Personal Information with each other to make sure your experience is as seamless as possible, and you have the full benefit of what Hooters has to offer. For example, if you select a Hooters franchise as your favorite Hooters location, that franchisee may be provided with Personal Information such as your contact information and birthday, in order to make special offers to you.
Hooters may share your Personal Information with non-HOA companies that perform services on our behalf, as necessary for them to perform those services. HOA requires those non-HOA companies to have reasonable safeguards in place to protect any Personal Information they may receive in a manner consistent with applicable law. Further, Hooters may share information collected by cookies and other automated means with sites you link to from the Sites or Mobile App, or social media providers (such as Twitter, Facebook, and Google+) that you visit by clicking through from our Sites or Mobile App. Please note that while Hooters does not capture or store your login information or any other personally identifiable information for Twitter, Facebook, and other social networks, their websites may store session information or cookies on your computer or wireless phone.
In Other Circumstances: Other than as noted above, we may provide Personal Information to non-HOA companies or other third parties for purposes such as:
• Complying with court orders and other legal processes; • To assist with identity verification, and to prevent fraud and identity theft; • Selling HOA Companies’ assets, or in process of arranging for the dissolution of the HOA Companies through bankruptcy; or • Enforcing our agreements and property rights.
