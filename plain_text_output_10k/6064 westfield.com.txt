INFORMATION WE COLLECT
Westfield may collect the following types of information from you when you use our Online Services or visit Westfield centers: 
Online Services.
Information you provide to us. When using our Online Services, you may provide information directly, such as by creating an account, filling out a form, signing up for SmartParking, completing a "contact us" query, applying for a career with Westfield, or signing up for newsletters or other communications. Additionally, you may choose to provide your location when using a Westfield Online Service.
Information we collect automatically from your device.  We may also collect information from you automatically when you use our Online Services. For instance, we may collect information about your use of our Online Services, including the time and duration of your visit, the referring URL, your Internet Protocol (IP) address, the type of device you use and its operating system, your advertising ID (e.g., AAID and IDFA), and other device identifiers.
Cookies and similar technologies. Westfield and its partners use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies when you use our Online Services. For more information, including how to manage your cookie preferences, see our Cookie Policy .
Competitions and Sweepstakes. When you enter a competition, sweepstakes, or register for an event you may provide contact information directly to us including name, address, email address, and/or phone number.
In Our Centers.
Location Information. We may use Bluetooth (BLE) Beacons, Wi-Fi, and similar technologies within some of our centers that may communicate with your (mobile) device and identify its location if the device has Bluetooth and/or Wi-Fi enabled and is properly configured. As a result, we may collect your Wi-Fi and/or Bluetooth MAC Address and send BLE signals to your device. To completely opt-out of BLE Beacons and/or the collection of your MAC address, turn off your device Bluetooth and Wi-Fi capabilities.  To learn more about mobile location analytics and to opt-out of mobile location tracking for companies that have agreed to the Mobile Location Analytics Code of Conduct please visit https://smart-places.org/ .
Westfield Wi-Fi. If you use one of the Wi-Fi networks in our centers, we will collect personal information from you in order to log you on to the network, including your name and email address. We may also collect information about your use of the Wi-Fi, such as the web pages you visit, your MAC or IP address, and your location while using the Wi-Fi.
Concierge Services. If you use our concierge services in center or online, we may collect information from you to facilitate the products or services you request. Concierge services vary by location--check with the concierge in your local center to see what services are available and what personal information is required to use those services.
Competitions and Sweepstakes. When you enter a competition, sweepstakes, or register for an event at one of our centers you may provide contact information directly to us including name, address, email address, and/or phone number. 
Third-Party Sources. Westfield may obtain information about you from third parties and/or link the information we collect from you to additional records made available by third parties.

Advertising. Westfield uses the information you provide us to help us tailor and show advertisements to you. We may partner with third parties to help us display relevant advertising and to manage our advertising across multiple channels. You may opt out of some interest-based cookie advertising by clicking " Cookie Settings " and for more information about third-party advertisers and your ability to opt-out of targeted advertising from such third-parties please visit the Network Advertising Initiative ( http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/ ) and the Digital Advertising Alliance ( http://www.aboutads.info/choices/ ).
